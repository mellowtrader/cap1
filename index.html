Best New Socks - Website Plan

Mission Statement

The goal is to create a website with the best user experience that guides customers to the perfect pair of socks for them.

There will be no need for searching, wondering about size or fit, or selecting anything beyond design.

This will be accomplished with a clean layout that poses a series of questions to the customer, with the answers narrowing the products that fit their specifications.

Sock Connoisseur

I've been wearing socks for over 40 years. I've worn ankle socks, knee socks, and everything in between. Some have been too thin. Some have been to thick and stuffy. Some have not held up, and some have cut off circulation.

Few have been perfect. The perfect socks I have found have been a blessing.

Needs List

Once created, the website will need...

  • a supplier of quality socks of all types. More than one supplier may need to be used. Need to research sock suppliers.
  • a checkout counter. I'll need a website host that works well with Shopify.

Once all that is accomplished, it'll be time to market and advertise!
